The Gorkhaly Foundation is a volunteer run non-profit organization of Washington D.C. metropolitan area in support of Young Nepalese Professionals. Over the past years Gorkhaly Foundation has partnered with over dozen of Grassroot organizations in USA and Nepal. The foundation in 2013, with NKY & COMMITTED, raised 35K for 12 NGOs in Nepal by organizing Walk for Nepal 2013 - platform for organizing walkathons in cities worldwide to mobilize individuals and organizations interested in the development of Nepal. In 2013, the foundation promoted CNN hero Puspa Basnet's documnetry "Waiting for Mamu" to support the butterfly Home project. Also in 2012, we raised funds for Community Members Interest Organization to building schools in Nepal by hosting a Xbox Football Tournament. Now we are dedicating the foundation to immediate and long term recovery of Nepal from the 2015 Earthquake. This will be the main mandate for the coming years.
