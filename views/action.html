Partnerships Overview

Under one umbrella of the Nepal Rising Campaign, the global partners in and outside of the United States come together with a common vision of a resilient Nepal. These local efforts contribute to the strategic goals of Nepal Rising through increasing local support to build resilient communities in Nepal for a sustainable future. Our partners currently include city-based teams in Austin, Dallas, Boston, Chicago, DC, and Nepal. We invite you to engage your community in this global movement for relief, rebuilding, and resiliency!
